Audi

Audi is a German luxury automobile manufacturer founded in 1909 by August Horch and currently headquartered in Ingolstadt, Germany. Now a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group, Audi is renowned for its slogan "Vorsprung durch Technik," which means "Advancement through Technology."

Audi's first car was the Audi Type A, produced in 1910 by the original Audi Automobilwerke GmbH, founded by August Horch.
